# References and Further Reading

This section gathers essential resources for those who wish to dive deeper into the concepts behind Genetic Algorithms (GAs), as well as related areas such as Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. It includes academic references, open-access libraries, scientific journals, and the monograph that led to the creation of the GADemo platform.

---

## Foundational Texts on Genetic Algorithms

- **“Genetic Algorithms in Search, Optimization, and Machine Learning” by David E. Goldberg**  
  This comprehensive book introduces the theory and application of genetic algorithms, serving as a cornerstone in the field.  
  [View on Google Books](https://www.google.com/books/edition/Genetic_Algorithms_in_Search_Optimization/BfovAQAAIAAJ)

- **“Algoritmos Genéticos” by Ricardo Linden**  
  [Publisher’s page](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/263124877_Algoritmos_Geneticos)


## 🎓 Some Academic Publications and Thesis

- **[Optimal Design of Building Structures Using Genetic Algorithms](https://thesis.library.caltech.edu/8132/)**
  Two special genetic algorithms, hGA and vGA, are presented for continuous and discrete optimization problems, respectively.

- **[Algoritmos Genéticos Aplicados à Análise de Jogos de Tabuleiro](https://repositorio.ufu.br/bitstream/123456789/39855/3/AlgoritmosGen%C3%A9ticosAplicados.pdf)**  
  Explores how GAs can model strategic decision-making in board games such as Monopoly.

- **[Fundamentos de Algoritmos Evolutivos (USP)](https://repositorio.usp.br/bitstreams/7472618b-87b3-4077-a1ca-eb5f40a0542c)**  
  A solid overview of evolutionary algorithms and their applications to complex problem solving.

- **[GADemo Undergraduate Thesis – PUC-Rio (Maxwell)](https://doi.org/10.17771/PUCRio.acad.68870)**  
  This monograph documents the design, development, and analysis of the GADemo platform as a final undergraduate project.

## Open-access Repositories and Digital Libraries

- **[CiteSeerX](https://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu)**  
  Scientific search engine and digital library focused on literature in computer science and information systems.

- **[Redalyc](https://redalyc.org)**  
  Network of scientific journals from Latin America, Spain, and Portugal offering free access to high-quality academic papers.

- **[DBLP](https://dblp.org)**
  Comprehensive bibliography covering computer science research articles and conference papers.

---

## Scientific Journals

- **[Applied Sciences – MDPI (AI & Robotics Section)](https://www.mdpi.com/journal/applsci/sections/ai_robotics)**  
  Peer-reviewed open access journal publishing practical applications of artificial intelligence, robotics, and evolutionary computation.

- **[Algorithms – MDPI](https://www.mdpi.com/search?q=genetic+algorithms&journal=algorithms)**  
  Focuses on the design, analysis, and application of algorithms, including genetic algorithms and other metaheuristics.

- **[Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/artificial-intelligence)**  
  A multidisciplinary journal exploring deep learning, neuroevolution, and intelligent systems, with all content freely accessible.

- **[PeerJ Computer Science](https://peerj.com/computer-science/)**  
  Open access journal covering computer vision, machine learning, and evolutionary algorithms with code-sharing incentives.

- **[PLOS Computational Biology](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/)**  
  High-impact open access journal connecting biology with computational intelligence, including applications of genetic algorithms and neural networks.
